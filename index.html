Role: Leader of the Pogues

Sarah Cameron

Image Details
Role: "Kook Princess"
Background: Sarah is the daughter of the Cameron family (Ward, Rose, Rafe, and Wheezie), and despite her background of being rich privileged living in Figure 8(rich/elite side), she aligns with the Pogues who lives on the cut (lower class/poor side of the city).

Kiara Carrera

Image Details
Role: Kook & Pogue
Background: Kiara is a passionate girl who stands up for what she believes in, often getting caught in the middle of the conflict between the Kooks and Pogues because her family is in between.

JJ Maybank

Image Details
Role: Pogue and Troublemaker of the group
Background: JJ is loyal and always looking for adventure. He's the "wild card" of the group, always getting in trouble with the police and enemies.

Pope Heyward

Image Details
Role: Pogue
Background: Pope is the smartest among the Pogues, who dreams of a better life and helping his family.

Rafe Cameron

Image Details
Role: Kook and Older Brother of Sarah

Cleo
